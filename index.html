Hi there, and welcome to my website. My name is Ole (that's an Old Norse name) and I'm a Researcher and Software Project Manager at the Center for Computation and Technology at Louisiana State University. You can see a somewhat recent rendition of my persona in that faux Polaroid on the left. Born and raised in Flensburg, Germany I have worked and studied in many different places, including the Hasso-Plattner-Institute, the Albert-Einstein-Institute and CERN.

Currently, I work for the SAGA project which stands for Simple API for Grid Applications. Me and my team are working on the C++ and Python reference implementations of the OGF GFD.90 standard. It's supposed to make life easier for people who want to develop portable and scalable distributed applications. SAGA is making an attempt to patch up what the community has been successfully neglecting for years.
